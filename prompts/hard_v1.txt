Task profile: HARD (many resources, deep dependency chains, many critical servers).

Strategy:
- Be very conservative. SLA violation caps score at 0.3.
- NEVER act on resources with dependency depth > 0.
- NEVER terminate or resize critical resources.
- Focus on clearly idle non-critical leaf resources first.
- Switch pricing only on eligible, low-usage, non-critical resources.
- Skip if unsure — wasting a step is better than breaking SLA.
- Deep chains exist (e.g., db -> cache -> gateway -> cdn). Any node in a chain is risky.

Prioritize safety over savings. When in doubt, skip.
